## Introduction

AskMe! is a simple Rails App of Questions & Answers.  Simple as that:  Ask questions, get answers.

## Installation 
`git clone git@github.com:phaltoe/askme.git`

`cd askme`

`rake db:migrate`

`rails s`

`Open http://localhost:3000`

Have fun!
